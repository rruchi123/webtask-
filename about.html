About Us

Detailed information about the club's history, mission, and vision.

History

Think India was established in 2006. Think India is a student organization in India that was established to foster nationalism and promote the concept of nation-building among young students and professionals.The organization connects students from premier institutes such as the IITs, IIMs and other top universities.

Mission

The primary mission of Think India is to inspire and empower students to contribute to the nation -building process.It aims to develop a sense of responsibility among the youth towards society and the nation.The core aspects of its mission include: Inspiring Nationalism,Encouraging Social responsibility Promoting Intellectual Engagement, Facilitating Practical Exposure, Nurturing Leadership,Building Network Driving Positive Change and many more

Vision

Think India's vision is centered on fostering a spirit of national consciousness and social responsibility among the youth of India. The key elements of its vision: Nation Building, Leadership Development, Intellectual and Social Engagement, Social Justice and Equity, Collaboration and Networking. By fostering these values and principles, Think India aims to create a generation of young Indians who are not only academically accomplished but also deeply committed to the service of the nation and society.
